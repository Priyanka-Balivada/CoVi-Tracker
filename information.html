India Fights With Corona

While India was flourishing and growing with its almighty, it was hit badly by Corona or Covid-19 and als! Everything came to a halt! The most affected was the middle class, but as we all know, India is a diversified country, we all live together and have always been there for each other, and hence for all the ones out there out of the national boundaries, we INDIANS are known for unity in diversity. This pandemic has taken away a lot of things from us. At times when everyone was trying to become wealthy and collect money, it was leart that money is not everything one needs to survive, it's rather the goodwill, the kindness and the loved ones. That came out to help. People from all races, caste, creed and religion came out for help to the needy. When the virus attacked, it took away even the riches, the celebrities and the common too. It didn't take away only the poors, so much money could not save the riches. But yess, after helping out each other, and following all the safety protocols at the personal level, we have come this far, and survived the pandemic with all our might,healthy habits and good governance. By maintaining safe distance, wearing masks, sanitizing places, following hygiene, we have come up to the present day and are still fighting corona. The doctors, the police, the healthcare workers, many lost their Lives, but none gave up,working even harder each time ... , to keep the citizens safe, truly they are the superheroes of mankind. By the time the second wave came most of the citizens were getting vaccinated, and as we all know our country has always been a peaceful nation, the government of India donated crores and crores of vaccines to other countries too as a token of goodwill under the mission VACCINE MAITRI. And for this Index has also come to be known as the producer of medicine. India has not just fought the pandemic within national boundaries but also helped fight against corona internationally. As our ancestors have taught VASUDHAIVA KUTUMBAKAM which means the whole universe as a family, our country India has proved it correctly! The citizens followed all the safety protocols, although there were problems as we are a developing nation, Covid-19 was something the world was unaware of, & took time and is still talking, but we have come a long way, and we will definitely win this war with CORONA definitely. Everyone came out to help each other, by donation, by imparting knowledge, by giving their own resources and in some other way.
